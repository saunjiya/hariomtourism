About Hari Om Tourism

Your Trusted Travel Partner in the Himalayas

Hari Om Tourism, based in the beautiful town of Pithoragarh, Uttarakhand, specializes in organizing spiritual, adventure, and nature tours across the Kumaon Himalayas.

We proudly offer professionally managed tour packages to Adi Kailash, Om Parvat, Darma Valley, Chaukori, Panchachuli, , and many more breathtaking destinations.
